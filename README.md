# A Song of Soil and Ink — Willas Tyrell SI Fanfiction

## Description

A four-year-old boy wakes from a fever that should have killed him. He can't remember his own name, but he remembers every page of a book that shouldn't exist — an encyclopedia of everything humanity learned over a thousand years of progress. Willas Tyrell, heir to the richest kingdom in Westeros, has no prophecies, no magic, and no idea what's coming — just soil, steel, patience, and twenty years before the world catches fire.

*They'll notice the food first. Then the roads. Then the steel. By the time they notice the rest, it won't matter.*

**Content Warnings:** Feudal morality, pragmatic protagonist, child soldiers (medieval context), political marriages, concubines (later volumes)

## About

A slow-burn ASOIAF kingdom-building fanfiction. A man dies, a boy wakes up — Willas Tyrell, age four, with no memory of his past life but perfect recall of The Book: a hand-illustrated encyclopedia of human achievement across agriculture, medicine, engineering, metallurgy, economics, and war.

- **Target chapter length:** 4,000-5,000 words (flexible)
